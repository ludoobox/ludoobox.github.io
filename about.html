My name is Ludovic, I'm 28 years old, I live in Paris and I'm a student in video game design and 3D animation.
I started my career in the food industry, then moved on to retail and finally decided to follow my true path of becoming an artist.

Creation has always been a big part of my life; I have, since I was a child, created worlds and characters that followed me growing up, helping me escape into my creativity. I would always be brainstorming ideas to make characters and games that I interacted with, better.

I've always seen the world in a filter of my own, and I'm proud to share my vision to the world.
My dream is to create video games which include the worlds that I've created and will create, to be able to share my love for art and my passion for video games.
